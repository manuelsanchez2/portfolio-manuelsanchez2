Welcome to Whiskeymaniacs

External Whiskey Data Base

Whiskeymaniacs started as a self challenge in order to put into practice what I have learned during the week on using existent APIs. By using the external data base on our app, you will be able to find some information about your favourite whiskey in almost no time.

Also one of the future implementations of the app could be setting up a wish list, so that you can also keep record of what are the best whiskies for you. See the whole repository here.
